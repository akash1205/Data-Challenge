Loan Strategy
The Loan Baskets are defined based on the following strategy:

  1. Extra Small : These are the loans which comes under the first quantile of the loan distribution. These are safe loans with a very low loan amount and thus, are less riskier. Range: (1 <= Loan_Amount_000 < 152)
  2. Small : These are the loans which comes under the interquantile region of the loan distribution. These are safe loans with a very semi moderate loan amount have high popularity and exhibit high revenue generation. Range : (152 <= Loan_Amount_000 <= 347)
  3. Medium : These are the loans which comes after the 3rd quantile region of the loan distribution. These have moderate loan amount and safe because majority of them come with the backing of being conventional and conforming. Range : (348 <= Loan_Amount_000 <= 550)
  4. Large : These are the loans which comes after the 3rd quantile region of the loan distribution. These have high loan amount and are comparitively riskier because inconjuction of being high on loan amount very few of them are conventional and conforming. Range : (Loan_Amount_000 > 551)

Analysis Based on the aggregated data for the given years, it is clear that Small loans have been originated the most, followed by Extra Small, Medium and Large. Small Loans have also been the most revenue genearting bucket which signifies that Small bucket loans are more favourable because of the senergy between risk and revenue. On drilling the buckets down by Loan Purpose, it is significant that Refinance Loan mortgages has been originated the most in all the buckets which makes them more favorable for any lending organization. On drilling the buckets down by Conventional Conforming Flag, it is clear that loans which are both conventional and conforming are the most approving loans because of them being backed by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac, as the lending institutions can sell their mortgages to Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac to reduce the risk.

Conclusion
Based on the data provided, it is safe to say that Change Financial should enter the Lending Market and should follow in the footsteps of the existing lenders in the market by focussing on Conventional and Conforming Refinance loans. This move will be suitable for them as the they are trying to enter a new market and since there is no data about their financial and lending capability, they should start with a segment which has the senergy between risk and revenue. They should also take into consideration the Purchase Loans with certain guidlines which should be set based on their financial and lending capability.
